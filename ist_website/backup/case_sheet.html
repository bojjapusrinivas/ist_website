Special Olympics

  • Special Olympics World Games 2019 was hosted in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates (UAE) during March 2019 for the first time in the Middle East & North Africa (MENA) International Region, since the movement’s founding over 50 years ago. It was the world’s largest humanitarian sporting event & a global movement, which focused on the empowerment of People of Determination with Intellectual Disabilities through the Power of Sport. Renowned for its visionary leadership, UAE had been at the forefront in the Middle East in the protection & promotion of rights for people with intellectual disabilities or people of determination.
  • iSpatial Techno Solutions, with support from the Abu Dhabi Smart Solutions & Services Authority (ADSSSA) had Designed & Developed the Web-based Geographic Information System (GIS) Applications, subsequently composed the Smart & Innovative Story Maps on Special Olympics World Games 2019.
  • For composition of Story Maps, the Special Olympics (SO) relevant Historical Data, Locations’ Information including Live Data Feeds (through Web Services) were sourced from SO UAE, Spatial Layers were provided by ADSDI, while hosting of the Story Maps was supported by ADSSSA
  • The ArcGIS Story Map for Special Olympics comprises of
    • Host Cities of Previous Special Olympics
    • Stadiums that hosted Special Olympics 2019
    • Games hosted in Specific Stadiums
    • 3D Tour of Stadiums
    • Live Tracking of Results & Winners

URL: https://apps.sdi.gov.abudhabi/specialolympics/
