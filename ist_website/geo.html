Spatial Data Infrastructure (SDI):

Implementing Spatial data infrastructure (SDI) framework is an effective way to ensure data and resources are available to the organizations and stakeholders that need them while at the same time reducing ongoing licensing costs.

iSpatialTec specializes in utilizing Esri SDI technology integrating with service-oriented architecture (SOA) principles that support existing IT, Web, and geospatial interoperability standards, including the industry specifications of the Open Geospatial Consortium, Inc.r (OGCr). iSpatialTec helps organizations in improving knowledge sharing, reducing duplication of effort, direct people toward the best available data, and improve the overall quality of geospatial data and information.

Our experience includes:
  • Federated roadmap development with stakeholder entities
  • Design and development of Data Management framework
  • Foundation technology platform and data clearinghouse implementation
  • Optimising GIS deployments for virtualised environments
  • Maintaining compatibility with legacy back office systems
  • Ensuring performance under heavy use
  • Providing industry compliant metadata management solutions
  • Designing database schemata that add value to your data
  • Helping clients pass security audits (e.g. PSN compliance)
  • Developing uncomplicated access control management systems based on Active Directory
